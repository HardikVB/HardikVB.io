Who am I?

I'm a multimedia engineering student and I enjoy many different things, I like to think that I do think outside the box (when sometimes I should think inside) I enjoy learning new skills and challenge myself in many aspects, right now I want to work in the Ux/Ui field of work, as I believe it is one of the most important aspects in the industry, we all are customers of any kind and the experience should be inclusive to everyone.

But who am I really?

Besides the "tradional" intro of myself, I do have many different passions and hobbies that I like to spend my free time (and to take a break from time to time). I spent most of my university summer holidays doing volunteering projects abroad (through the European Solidarity Corps), those experiences helped me to develop myself as individual, helping me getting out of my comfort zone, developing communication and teamworking skills and many other things.

I also enjoy photography, it allows me to be creative in my own way and capture the world as I see it through my eye. As for sports, I started kickboxing in 2022, having had the chance to compete already, first in the regionals and then qualifying myself for the nationals.
